about selfie_nyc

selfie_nyc is a website that lets users take selfies using live NYCDOT traffic cameras.


NYCDOT employs the use of over 600 traffic cameras located around the city in order to monitor the real-time flow of traffic. A live feed for each of these cameras is publically available at NYCDOT's website. By scraping this feed and placing each camera on a map, selfie_nyc lets users easily navigate to and download self portraits using these cameras.


This project began as a way to contrast the inherent sense of agency involved in snapping a selfie on one's own device, with the reality of near-constant government surveillence throughout the city.
